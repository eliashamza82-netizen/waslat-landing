Everything you need to connect, automate, and grow your business using the Waslat platform.

Introduction

Waslat is an all-in-one business operating system for marketing agencies and SMEs in the MENA region. It brings together social media management, customer conversations, AI automation, CRM, and analytics into a single powerful dashboard.

With Waslat you can connect all your social media channels, manage customer messages in one inbox, schedule and publish content, and deploy AI agents that work 24/7 on your behalf.

Quick Start

1

Create your account

Go to app.waslat.io/register and sign up. You get a free 14-day trial with full access.

2

Connect your channels

Navigate to Integrations in the sidebar and connect your social media accounts and messaging channels.

3

Set up your inbox

Go to Conversations to start receiving and replying to messages from all connected channels in one place.

4

Enable AI agents

Visit AI Agents to activate autonomous agents that handle inbox replies, content creation, and more.

Account Setup

After signing up, complete your profile by adding your business name, logo, and timezone. If you are an agency, you can create subaccounts for each of your clients under Settings → Subaccounts.

WhatsApp Business

Requirements

  • A WhatsApp Business account
  • A verified Facebook Business Manager account
  • A dedicated phone number not already on WhatsApp

How to connect

1

Go to Integrations → WhatsApp Business and click Connect.

2

Log in with your Facebook account and select your Business Manager.

3

Select or create a WhatsApp Business Account and add your phone number.

4

Verify your phone number via OTP and complete setup.

Features available

  • Send and receive messages in the Conversations inbox
  • Schedule WhatsApp messages
  • Automated replies via AI InboxAgent
  • Broadcast messages to contact lists
  • Media sharing: images, videos, documents

Telegram

Requirements

  • A Telegram Bot created via @BotFather
  • Bot token from BotFather

How to connect

1

Open Telegram and message @BotFather. Send /newbot and follow the prompts to create your bot.

2

Copy the Bot Token provided by BotFather.

3

Go to Integrations → Telegram, paste your Bot Token and click Connect.

Features available

  • Send messages to Telegram chats and channels
  • Receive incoming messages in the Conversations inbox
  • Reply to customers via Telegram bot
  • Send images and media

💡 Your customers must message your bot first before you can send them messages — this is a Telegram policy.

White Label

Agencies can white label Waslat under their own brand. Go to Settings → White Label to configure:

  • Custom logo and brand colors
  • Custom platform name
  • Custom domain (e.g. app.youragency.com)
  • Client login portal with your branding

Webhooks

Waslat supports incoming webhooks from all connected platforms. Webhooks are automatically configured when you connect a channel. For custom webhook endpoints contact support.

Billing & Subscription

Your subscription is managed under Settings → Billing & Subscription. You can view your current plan, usage, and payment history. Waslat offers a 14-day free trial with no credit card required. Contact sales@waslat.io for pricing.

FAQ

Can I connect multiple accounts per platform?

Yes — each subaccount can have its own connected channels. Agencies can manage multiple clients each with their own integrations.

Is my data secure?

Yes. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Waslat uses Firebase with strict security rules and never stores raw access tokens in plain text.

What happens after my trial ends?

Your account will be paused until you subscribe. Your data is retained for 30 days. Contact sales@waslat.io to continue.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes — cancel anytime from Settings → Billing with no penalties.

Do you support Arabic?

Yes — Waslat is fully bilingual in Arabic and English. Switch languages from the top right of the dashboard.
